        JavaScript (Zero Fill) Bitwise Left Shift (<<)
        One or more zero bits are pushed in from the right, and the leftmost 
        bits fall off
        5       00000000000000000000000000000101
        5 << 1  00000000000000000000000000001010 (10)
        5 << 2  00000000000000000000000000010100   parseInt(10100,2)= (20)
        5 << 3  00000000000000000000000000101000   parseInt(101000,2) bin to dec= (40)

    shift use to multiply or divide by 2^m where m is integer 1,2,3,4...8
  ///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
        a << m = (a x 2^m)
        5 << 1       
        solution 
        1. convert 5 from decimal to binary 101
        2. << 1 mean add one zero to the right most of the binary value 1010
        3. convert the remaining binary to decimal

        so 5 << 4  = 1010000 =>80
  //////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
    (a / 2^m)
     90 >> 1
      solution 
      1. convert 90 from decimal to binary 1011010
      2. >> 1 mean drop one right most bit(digit) so >> 3 is drop 3 right most digit
      3. convert the remaining binary value to decimal 
    90 >> 4  (90 in bin is 1011010)
    and drop 4 right most digit, the remaining is 1011
